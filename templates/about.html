About PlantCare AI

PlantCare AI is designed to support modern agriculture by providing fast and reliable plant disease classification from leaf images. The platform helps reduce diagnosis time and enables timely treatment decisions.

Mission

Our mission is to make AI-driven plant health diagnostics accessible for farmers, home gardeners, and agricultural learners through a simple and practical interface.

Technology Stack

  • Transfer Learning with MobileNetV2
  • Fine-tuned model trained on 87,000+ annotated images
  • 38 plant disease classes including healthy conditions
  • Flask backend with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frontend

Why Transfer Learning?

Transfer learning reuses useful visual features learned from large image datasets, improving model performance while reducing training time and computational cost.
